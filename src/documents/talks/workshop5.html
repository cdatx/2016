Introduction to Mesos

Instructor: I'm Alan Scherger (@flyinprogrammer), I spend most of my days building out the future multi-paas platform of HomeAway, and in my spare time I attempt to contribute to a variety of open source projects.

Alan will go over the basics of Mesos, teach you how to build a mesos cluster, avoid the most common pitfalls while building a mesos cluster. Finally, Alan will cover best practices of how to run a cluster in a production setting.
